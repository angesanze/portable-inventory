# Daily Usage Guide

This guide is for warehouse workers and managers using the Varasto dashboard.

## Logging In
1. Navigate to the main dashboard at `http://localhost`.
2. Enter your **Username** and **Password**.
   - *Note: If your company provided a License Code, ensure you have your specific credentials.*

## Dashboard Overview
Once logged in, you will see the **Command Deck**, which gives you a quick overview of:
- Total Products in stock.
- Low stock alerts.
- Recent movements.

## Managing Inventory

### 1. Product Models
Models are the "blueprints" of your items (e.g., "Drill Driver X200").
- Go to **Product Models**.
- Click **Create** to add a new type of item to the catalog.
- Add details like Name, Description, and Category.

### 2. Physical Products
These are the actual items on your shelf.
- Go to **Stock** or **Physical Products**.
- Click **Add Item**.
- Select the **Product Model**.
- Enter the **Serial Number** and select a **Location**.
- Status defaults to "In Stock".

### 3. Move Items
To render items or move them between locations:
- Go to **Movements**.
- Create a new movement record (e.g., "Stock Out" to a job site, or "Transfer" to another shelf).

## Locations
Manage where items are stored.
- Go to **Locations**.
- Define warehouses, rooms, or specific shelves.
